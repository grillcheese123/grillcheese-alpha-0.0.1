# GrillCheese AI - Project Description

## What It Is

GrillCheese is a **local AI assistant that remembers you**. Unlike ChatGPT or other cloud AI services, GrillCheese runs entirely on your computer and builds a permanent memory of your conversations, preferences, and context.

## The Problem

Current AI assistants forget everything:
- Every conversation starts from scratch
- You repeat yourself constantly ("I'm allergic to peanuts", "I prefer Python", "My company is...")
- Your data goes to cloud servers (privacy risk)
- Costs $20/month forever
- Stops working without internet
- Requires expensive new devices ($1000+ phones)

## The Solution

GrillCheese gives you:
- **Persistent Memory**: Remembers everything across all conversations
- **100% Private**: Runs on your device, never touches the cloud
- **Works Offline**: No internet required after setup
- **Free Forever**: No subscriptions, pay once (or free open source)
- **Runs on Old Hardware**: Works on 2021+ computers and phones

## How It Works

**Bio-Inspired Memory System:**
- Uses brain-like neural networks (spiking neurons) to form memories
- Stores conversations like your hippocampus stores experiences
- Retrieves relevant memories when you need them
- Learns your preferences over time

**Local Inference:**
- Runs Microsoft's Phi-3 language model on your GPU
- 20-30 tokens/second on budget GPUs
- Works on AMD, NVIDIA, Intel graphics
- Phone-capable (future)

## Technical Innovation

**Spiking Neural Networks (SNN):**
- Brain-inspired computing (like actual neurons)
- 75 custom GPU shaders for AMD/NVIDIA/Intel
- 10-100x more energy efficient than traditional AI
- Enables live learning (not just static responses)

**Hybrid Architecture:**
- SNN handles memory formation and retrieval
- Traditional neural network (Phi-3) handles language
- Bridge between the two = unique capability
- No one else has this combination
